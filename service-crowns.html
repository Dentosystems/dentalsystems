Crowns & Bridges

Restorative solutions for a complete, functional smile.

Returns functionality and aesthetics to damaged or missing teeth.

Dental Crowns

A crown is a "cap" that encircles a tooth or dental implant. Crowns are often needed when a large cavity threatens the health of a tooth. They are bonded to the tooth using dental cement.

Dental Bridges

A bridge is used to replace missing teeth by joining an artificial tooth permanently to adjacent teeth or dental implants.
