Tooth Extractions

Gentle and professional removal of problematic teeth.

While we always strive to save your natural teeth, sometimes an extraction is the best option for your overall oral health. Whether it's due to severe decay, impact (like wisdom teeth), or to make room for orthodontic treatment, we ensure the procedure is as comfortable as possible.

When is an Extraction necessary?

  • Severe tooth decay or infection.
  • Advanced gum disease.
  • Impacted wisdom teeth.
  • To prepare for orthodontic treatment (braces).

Aftercare Care

We provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure a quick and smooth recovery. Our team is always available to answer any questions you have during your healing process.
