Dental Fillings

Restoring decayed teeth with durable, natural-looking materials.

Dental fillings are used to repair minimal tooth fractures, tooth decay or otherwise damaged surfaces of the teeth. At Dent-o-care, we specialize in composite fillings, which are made of a tooth-colored resin that blends seamlessly with your natural smile.

Why choose Composite Fillings?

  • Aesthetics: The color can be matched to your existing teeth.
  • Bonding: Composite fillings actually bond to the tooth structure, providing further support.
  • Versatility: Can be used to repair chipped, broken, or worn teeth.
