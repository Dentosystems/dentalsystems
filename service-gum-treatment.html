Gum Treatment

Ensuring the foundation of your smile is healthy and strong.

Gum disease (periodontal disease) is an infection of the tissues that support your teeth. It is a leading cause of tooth loss in adults. At Dent-o-care, we provide comprehensive care to prevent and treat gum disease at all stages.

Our Periodontal Services:

  • Root Planing: Deep cleaning to remove tartar from below the gum line.
  • Polishing & Prophylaxis: Routine maintenance to keep gums healthy.
  • Gingival Care: Specialized treatments for inflamed or bleeding gums.

Why healthy gums matter?

Healthy gums provide the necessary support for your teeth. Neglecting gum health can lead to bone loss and eventually tooth loss. Our team is here to help you maintain a healthy mouth for life.
