Oral Cancer Screening

Early detection is crucial. Protect your health with our comprehensive screening services.

Oral cancer screening is a vital preventive service that can detect early signs of oral cancer and other serious oral conditions. At Dent-o-clinic, we use advanced screening techniques to identify potential issues before they become serious.

Why is Oral Cancer Screening Important?

  • Early Detection: Finding oral cancer in its early stages dramatically improves treatment outcomes.
  • Painless Procedure: Our screening is quick, non-invasive, and painless.
  • Peace of Mind: Regular screenings give you confidence in your oral health.
  • Risk Assessment: We evaluate risk factors and provide personalized prevention advice.

What to Expect During Screening

During your oral cancer screening, we will:

  • Examine your mouth, throat, and jaw for any suspicious lesions or abnormalities
  • Check lymph nodes in your neck
  • Discuss risk factors and family history
  • Recommend follow-up treatment if necessary
