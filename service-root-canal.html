Root Canal Treatment

Saving your natural smile with precision and care.

Root canal treatment (also known as endodontic treatment) is a dental procedure used to treat infection at the center of a tooth. It is not painful and can save a tooth that might otherwise have to be removed.

Why do I need a Root Canal?

The infection is caused by bacteria that invade the tooth. This can happen after:

  • Tooth decay
  • Leaky fillings
  • Damage to teeth as a result of trauma, such as a fall

The Procedure

At Dent-o-care, we use the latest technology to ensure your treatment is comfortable and efficient. The infected pulp is removed, and the inside of the tooth is cleaned and sealed.
